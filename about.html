Who’s This Curious Human? 👀

Okay, those lines above were already super fun — but hey, this part’s going to get even better! 😉 So, here’s a little peek into my world the story behind the smile and the spark!

I’m Aishwarya a cheerful, curious human who finds joy in the tiniest things. I grew up in a cozy town filled with laughter, rain, and people who never let you go hungry 🌦️🍛. My school days? Full of memories, mischief, and way too many notebooks with doodles in the margins!

Fun fact: in 10th grade, my absolute favorite thing ever was the Hibiscus flower diagram 🌺. I could draw it like a pro those petals, stamens, and all! That’s where my love for biology truly bloomed. I was *the* biology girl always curious about how living things worked.

After 10th, I had my heart set on becoming a doctor 🩺 biology was my world, and I genuinely loved it! But life had other plans 😅. When it came time to choose my stream, I went with PCMCS (Physics, Chemistry, Math, and Computer Science). That’s where things took a turn — I slowly found myself enjoying coding more than chemical equations! 💻 What started as curiosity soon became passion — I realized I loved the way computers think, and how logic could create something real. So yeah, I guess I traded the stethoscope for a keyboard... and honestly, no regrets at all 😎✨.

Now, I’m happily living the techie life — exploring AI, experimenting with code, and occasionally yelling “why won’t this run?!” at my laptop (in a very loving way, of course 😂).

Outside the tech bubble, I’m all about music, journaling random thoughts, and finding joy in the simplest moments 🎧📖✨. And yes — I can communicate in five languages! Not perfectly fluent in all, but enough to make friends, order food, and survive anywhere on Earth 🌍😄.

So yep — that’s me! A curious blend of biology roots, tech dreams, and a whole lot of heart 💕. Thanks for sticking around to read — hope you smiled at least once!
